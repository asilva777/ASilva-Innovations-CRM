App Overview

App Name: ASilva Innovations CRM

Version: 1.000005

Short Description: ASilva CRM is a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) app tailored by ASilva Innovations to help businesses manage customer needs and interactions effectively. It focuses on enhancing resilience by providing tools for tracking contacts, managing deals, and analyzing customer interactions.

Category: Business Solution

Function: Sales & CRM

Industry: Business Services

Purpose: Organize contacts, opportunities, quotes, and products in a common CRM layout, with contact companies plotted on a map.

CRM Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Dashboard with an overview of activities, tasks, and key metrics.
  • Contacts Management: Add, edit, and delete contacts with updated information.
  • Deals Management: Add new deals, update statuses, and close deals when completed.
  • Tasks and Activities: Create tasks, assign to contacts/deals, set due dates, and notes.
  • Analytics and Reporting: Generate reports and view analytics to track key metrics and trends.
  • Navigation: Includes Home, Solutions, Resources, Services, Blog, About Us, and Contact Us.
